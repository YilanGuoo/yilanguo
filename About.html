Hi, this is
Yilan Guo

I'm currently a second-year M.A. student in Emerging Media Studies at BU COM. I earned my B.A. in Journalism from Beijing Sport University in China.
My research focuses on media effects. Specifically, I'm interested in how interactive and immersive media (e.g. video games, VR/AR) impact cognition and behavior, as well as how people use media and process information through emerging communication technology and social media. My research is grounded in communication and media psychology theories, and I employ experiments, surveys, and computational methods as part of my methodology. 
(If you're interested in collaboration, please email me at: yilanguo@bu.edu)
